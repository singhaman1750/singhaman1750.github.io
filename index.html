Aman Singh

I am a PhD researcher in Robotics at the IISc, Bengaluru, working in the Stochastic Robotics Lab under the Centre for Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS), advised by Dr. Shishir N Y Kolathaya.

I completed my B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Roorkee, where I developed a strong interest in mechanical design, dynamics, and robotics, which led me toward research-driven hardware development.

My research focuses on robotic actuator design, planetary gearbox optimization, and hardware-centric co-design for legged robots. I work on building practical, reproducible systems, combining computational optimization, automated CAD, and experimental validation.
